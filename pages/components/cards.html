5. Custom Cards ‚Äì Power of MadenCSS

One of the strongest features of MadenCSS is its layered architecture. Thanks to the @layer design, existing components can be safely extended or overridden without breaking the core system files..

By combining the base .card component with utility classes, you can create fully custom card designs while still relying on the original structure. No additional CSS files or custom styles are required.

This is the power of MadenCSS.
